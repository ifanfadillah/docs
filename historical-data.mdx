---
title: "Historical Data"
---

The Historical Data tab serves as a transparency tool, showing you the track record of the inventory based on past media buys.

![Historical Data Overview](/images/historical-data-overview.png)

Based on the **Historical Data Overview** above, here is a detailed explanation of each section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Display Historical Timeline">
    This left-side panel displays a chronological, scrolling list of past campaigns that have utilized this specific billboard.

    ![Historical Timeline](/images/historical-timeline.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Individual Campaign Record (Proof of Play)">
    This highlights a specific past campaign entry, detailing the brand name (e.g., "Para Perasuk"), the industry category, and the date it was active.

    ![Historical Campaign Record](/images/historical-campaign-record.png)

    By clicking on any of the past campaigns listed on the left side, a pop-up modal will appear showing a real-world photo of that exact advertisement playing on the physical screen. This provides visual evidence and gives you absolute confidence in the screen's visibility and quality.

    ![Detail Pop](/images/detail-pop.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Timeline Filter">
    You can use these buttons to filter the historical data to show campaigns from the last 3 Months, 6 Months, or 12 Months.

    ![Historical Timeline Filter](/images/historical-timeline-filter.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Total Industry">
    A quick summary metric that displays the total number of distinct industry categories (e.g., "8") that have advertised on this screen during your selected time period.

    ![Historical Total Industry](/images/historical-total-industry.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Contribution Aggregate">
    A dynamic doughnut chart that categorizes previous advertisers by industry (e.g., Travel & Hospitality, Entertainment & Media, Corporate).

    ![Historical Brand Contribution Aggregate](/images/historical-brand-contribution-aggregate.png)

    Clicking on a specific slice reveals exactly how many campaigns from that sector have utilized the screen.

    ![Detail Brand Contribution Aggregate](/images/detail-brand-contribution-aggregate.png)

    <Info>
      All Brand Contribution grouping based on Industry & Brand Taxonomy (see Industry & Brand Taxonomy).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
