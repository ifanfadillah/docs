---
title: "Zoom Level"
---

The Explore map utilizes a progressive zooming system that helps you navigate smoothly from an overview down to highly specific street-level inventory. Understanding these zoom thresholds will help you browse inventories more efficiently.

Here is how the zoom levels behave as you interact with the map:

## Default View (Zoom Level 4)

When you first open the Explore page, the map defaults to Zoom Level 4, providing a wide, macro-level view of the available inventories across regions.

![Zoom Level 4](/images/zoom-level-4.png)

---

## Cluster Click Progression

When you click on an inventory cluster on the map, the system will automatically zoom in by increments of \+3 levels to drill down into that specific area. For example, clicking a cluster at Level 4 will zoom the map to **Level 7.**

![Zoom Level 7 1](/images/zoom-level-7-1.png)

And clicking again will zoom it to **Level 10**.

![Zoom Level 10](/images/zoom-level-10.png)

---

## Inventory List Trigger

As you continue to zoom in, **Level 13** acts as a crucial threshold. Once the map reaches this level, the detailed inventory list will automatically appear on the left side of your screen, displaying the individual billboard cards for the assets in your current view.

![Zoom Level 13](/images/zoom-level-13.png)

---

## Maximum Zoom

The map allows you to drill all the way down to **Zoom Level 22**. This is the maximum street-level view, providing the closest possible look at the physical location and surrounding context of a specific billboard.

![Zoom Level 21](/images/zoom-level-21.png)
