---
title: "Shortlist Status"
---

There are three statuses that a shortlist can have, including:

## In Progress

This status indicates that your shortlist is currently active and you are still in the planning phase. At this stage, you are actively exploring and adding inventories to strategically group and organize your selected screens before finalizing your media mix.

  src="/images/shortlist-status-in-progress.png"
  alt="Shortlist Status In Progress"
  title="Shortlist Status In Progress"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>

---

## Under Review

This status is triggered when you have exported your shortlist into a proposal. It indicates that your curated list of inventories is currently being shared with clients or internal stakeholders for review, feedback, and approval.

<img
  src="/images/shortlist-status-under-review.png"
  alt="Shortlist Status Under Review"
  title="Shortlist Status Under Review"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>

---

## Completed

This status indicates that the shortlist proposal has received final client approval. The grouped inventories are now finalized and ready to be seamlessly converted into an active, live media plan for your new campaign.

<img
  src="/images/shortlist-status-completed.png"
  alt="Shortlist Status Completed"
  title="Shortlist Status Completed"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>

<Info>
  Please note that the changes to all these status stages do not happen automatically. Every status update (from _In Progress_, _Under Review_, to _Completed_) must be changed manually by the user according to the actual progress of your campaign planning.
</Info>
