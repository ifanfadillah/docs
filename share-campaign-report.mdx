title: "Share Campaign Report"
---

Another way you can use (see Download Campaign Report) to give clients access to view Campaign Reports is to provide a link from the campaign report web page (URL link) that they can access through a browser with internet support. 

To get the URL link and keyword (which will later be given to clients), you only need to press “**Share**” on the report page and a pop-up modal will appear asking you to enter the keyword from your account.This page will help you in sharing campaign reports.

Here are the steps to share your campaign report:

## Step 1: Click the "Share" Button

While viewing the **Reports** tab within your campaign details, locate and click the **Share** button positioned at the top right corner of the dashboard.

![Share Report 1](/images/share-report-1.png)

---

## Step 2: Set a Password

![Share Report 2](/images/share-report-2.png)

To ensure your campaign data remains private and secure, the system requires you to set up password protection for the shared link.  _In the "Share Report" modal that appears, enter a secure password of your choosing into the text field._ 

---

## Step 3: Copy and Share the Credentials

Once submitted, the system will generate the access portal for your report. The modal will now display the necessary sharing details: 

- **Campaign ID:** The unique system identifier for your campaign. 
- **URL Link:** The direct web address that stakeholders will use to access the interactive report. 
- **Password:** The password you just created, which the recipient will need to log in. 
- **Show Audience Demographics:** An optional toggle. Check this box if you want the shared dashboard to include demographic data profiles. 

Click the purple Copy to Clipboard button to quickly copy the URL and password. You can now paste these credentials into an email, document, or messaging app to securely share the report with your clients.
