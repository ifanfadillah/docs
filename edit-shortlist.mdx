title: "Edit Shortlist"
---

If there are changes to the basic information in your shortlist, you can change or edit it by following these steps:

## Step 1: Click the "Action" Button & Click "Edit"

![Edit Shortlist 1](/images/edit-shortlist-1.png)

This will display the shortlist information form (such as List Name, Created by Description) that will be changed or edited.

![Edit Shortlist 1 2](/images/edit-shortlist-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Click the “Save & Continue” Button

Once you have finished making changes, click the **“Save & Continue”** button to save the changed data.

![Edit Shortlist 2](/images/edit-shortlist-2.png)

You will then receive a notification that the shortlist data has been successfully edited.
