**Reports** are a core feature on the Playlog platform that provide comprehensive performance tracking for your Out-of-Home (OOH) campaigns. Acting as the central source of truth for media buyers and agencies, this feature translates complex, hyper-local mobility data into clear, actionable business insights.

Whether a campaign is actively running or has already concluded, you can utilize this feature to obtain detailed reports on your actual real-world delivery—ranging from daily impressions and rich audience demographics to verified Proof of Play.

This page will help you understand the various sections and data points available within the Campaign Reports feature.

## Playlog Measurement

The foundation of OOH measurement globally relies on three primary metrics. These metrics interact with each other to determine the total campaign delivery, which is calculated simply as: **Reach x Frequency = Impression**.

![Measurement Metrics 1](/images/measurement-metrics-1.png)

### Reach

The number of unique individuals that will likely see your ads regardless of frequency.

**Example:** Since 100 different people passed through the area, the **Reach = 10,000**.

### Frequency

Is the average number of times the ads are seen by the unique population reached.

**Example:** Because the average audience see the billboard 4 times, the **Frequency = 4**.

### Impression

The total number of exposures to your advertisement.

**Example (Calculation):**

- Impression = Reach x Frequency
- Impression = 100 (people) x 4 (views)
- **Impression = 4,000**
