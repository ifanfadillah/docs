---
title: "Export Proposal"
---

Once you are satisfied with the grouped inventory in your Shortlist, you can easily export this curated list into a comprehensive, professional media proposal\[cite: 483\]. This exported proposal can then be seamlessly shared with your clients or internal stakeholders for review, feedback, and final approval\[cite: 484\].

## Step 1: Click "Export Proposal"

![Export To Proposal 1](/images/export-to-proposal-1.png)

After clicking the **Export Proposal** button, the system will show the Export Proposals modal.

![Export To Proposal 1 2](/images/export-to-proposal-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Select Template

![Export To Proposal 2 2](/images/export-to-proposal-2.2.png)

From the **Template** dropdown menu, you must select the specific layout or format for your downloaded document. This is a mandatory step to ensure the generated file suits your specific presentation needs.

---

## Step 3: Select Checkbox Options

![Export To Proposal 3 1](/images/export-to-proposal-3.1.png)

Next, customize the data included in your proposal by checking the relevant options.

- **Show audience demographic:** Select this checkbox to include detailed data profiles of the audience that typically passes by your selected screens.
- **Show inventory per city:** Select this checkbox to organize and display your chosen billboards geographically by city.
- **Show traffic data on map:** Select this checkbox to include visual mobility and traffic density overlays in your document.

<Note>
  Select “Show inventory per city” first before showing traffic data on the map.
</Note>

---

## Step 4: Click Download

![Export To Proposal 4](/images/export-to-proposal-4.png)

Once you have configured your template and data options, use the action buttons at the bottom of the modal to successfully download.
