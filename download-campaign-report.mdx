---
title: "Download Campaign Report"
---

When your client requests a performance report for a campaign that has been run, you can provide a **PPT document** that you can download at any time. You can download the Campaign Report to give to the clients who ran campaigns with you. The current supported download format for the report document is the **PPT document** format.

You can organize the data displayed in the report based on the desired **date range**. For example, if a campaign runs for one month, but the client suddenly wants to know the progress of their campaign during the first week after it started, you can apply that filter to the Campaign Report and only display the data for that one week (out of the entire total period).

Here are the steps to download your campaign report:

## Step 1: Click "Download" Button

On the **Reports** tab within your campaign details, find and click the **Download** button located in the top right corner of the screen. 

![Download Report 1](/images/download-report-1.png)

This action will open the Download Report settings modal.

![Download Report 1 2](/images/download-report-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Set the Date Range

![Download Report 2](/images/download-report-2.png)

In the Download Report modal, click the **Date Range** field to specify the exact period of the campaign performance data you want to pull. Set the appropriate start and end dates.

---

## Step 3: Select a Report Template

![Download Report 3](/images/download-report-3.png)

---

## Step 4: Customize Additional Data Options

![Download Report 4](/images/download-report-4.png)

Next, you can customize what specific information will be included in the generated presentation document by checking the following options: 

- **Split report per billboard:** Select this option if you want to separate the performance data onto individual pages for each specific billboard screen. 
- **Show audience demographic:** Check this option to include the detailed demographic data profile of the audience that was exposed to your campaign. 
- **Show conclusion report:** Check this option to include a summarized campaign conclusion report at the end of the document.

<Note>
  If you check the **Show conclusion report** option, a new dropdown menu named **Summary Options** will appear. You must select how you want this summary to be grouped: by _Summary per Billboard_, _Summary per Week_, or _Summary per Month_.

  ![Download Report 4 2](/images/download-report-4.2.png)
</Note>

---

## Step 5: Click "Download"

![Download Report 5](/images/download-report-5.png)

Once all the parameters and data options have been configured to your needs, click the purple **Download** button at the bottom of the modal. The system will process your data and save the presentation document file (.ppt) directly to your device.
