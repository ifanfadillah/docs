---
title: "Edit Inventory Rate Card to Shortlist"
---

When organizing your shortlist, you may need to adjust the estimated pricing or booking duration for a specific screen to better reflect negotiated rates or specific campaign flights. The **Edit Rate Card** feature allows you to modify these details directly within your workspace.

<Note>
  Please be advised that the recent changes made are just temporary and will not have any effect on the master inventory data. These modifications only apply locally to the inventory saved within this specific shortlist.
</Note>

## Step 1: Click the Edit Icon

Locate the specific inventory card you want to modify within your shortlist. Click the **Edit Rate Card** action button (represented by a pencil icon) located on the right side of the card. This allows you to edit the rate card and modify the specific settings or booking details for that individual screen.

![Edit Rate Card 1](/images/edit-rate-card-1.png)

---

## Step 2: Adjust Rate Card Details

The "Edit Rate Card" modal will appear. Here, you can adjust the following parameters to fit your media plan: 

**Price:** Modify the estimated cost for the inventory in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). 

![Edit Rate Card 2](/images/edit-rate-card-2.png)

**Duration:** Enter the numerical length of the desired booking. 

**Duration Unit:** Use the dropdown menu to select the specific unit of time for your booking, choosing between **Day** or **Month**.

![Edit Rate Card 2 2](/images/edit-rate-card-2.2.png)

---

## Step 3: Save Changes

Once you have adjusted the price and duration to fit your campaign parameters, click the purple **Save** button to apply the temporary changes to your shortlist. 

![Edit Rate Card 3](/images/edit-rate-card-3.png)
