---
title: "Create Shortlist"
---

Creating a shortlist is the foundational step where your media planning begins to take shape. As an Agency or Media Buyer, this is a dedicated workspace where you will strategically group your selected inventory, compare estimated costs, and refine your overall media mix before executing your final ad buy or exporting a proposal. Before you begin, ensure you have a general idea of your campaign goals, target audience, and desired geographic locations to guide your exploration.

There are several steps involved in creating a shortlist on Playlog:

## Step 1: Click "Create New" Shortlist

![Create New Shortlist 1](/images/create-new-shortlist-1.png)

After clicking the **“Create New”** Shortlist Button, the system will show the modal form to fill in information about the shortlist you will create.

![Create New Shortlist 1 2](/images/create-new-shortlist-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Fill Shortlist Information

![Create New Shortlist 2](/images/create-new-shortlist-2.png)

Based on the form above, the information or data required to create a new campaign is as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="List Name (Required)">
    This is the primary identifier for your Shortlist. The placeholder text suggests a structured naming convention, such as "Brand Name - Agency Name - Media Type," which helps you keep your workspace organized and easily searchable.

    <Info>
      The shortlist name you use cannot be duplicated. When you enter a shortlist name that has been used previously and still exists in the system, the system will issue a warning that the shortlist name cannot be used.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Created by (Required)">
    This field identifies the user or team member responsible for generating the list. You should enter your full name here to maintain clear accountability within your agency or team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Description (Optional)">
    A larger text area where you can add a short description about the list. You can use this to outline the campaign's goals, target audience, or any specific notes related to the media mix you plan to build.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 3: Save & Continue

Once you have verified the Basic Information, click the **Save & Continue** button at the bottom of the page.

![Create New Shortlist 3](/images/create-new-shortlist-3.png)

This will save your progress (**Status: “In Progress”)** and move you to the next phase to see Add Inventory to Shortlist (see Add Inventory to Shortlist).
