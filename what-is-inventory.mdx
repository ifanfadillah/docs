title: "What is Inventory"
---

In the Playlog ecosystem, the **Inventory Display** feature serves as a strategic discovery tool for Media Buyers, Agencies, and Direct Advertisers. Instead of managing owned assets, this feature allows you to seamlessly explore and evaluate available Out-of-Home (OOH) and Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) screens across the network.

Through this feature, you can see the specification ofinventory, analyze real-world performance metrics, and add to shortlist . This page helps you understand the various menus and data points available within the Inventory detail.

## Acessing Inventory Detail

You can access the Inventory Detail by click the **"See Details"** Button in the Inventory Card.

![Accessing Inventory Detail Page 1](/images/accessing-inventory-detail-page-1.png)

Then it will display the **Inventory Detail Page.**

![Accessing Inventory Detail Page 1 2](/images/accessing-inventory-detail-page-1.2.png)

## Inventory Detail Overview

![Inventory Detail Overview 1 1](/images/inventory-detail-overview-1-1.png)

Based on the **Inventory Detail Overview** above, here is a detailed explanation of each section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Inventory Photo">
    This section displays high-resolution photos of the billboard, giving you a detailed view of the asset and its surrounding environment to help evaluate visibility.

    ![Inventory Photo](/images/inventory-photo.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inventory Name & Location">
    Displays the name of the billboard (e.g., LED Lamp Pole GBK - Jl. Pintu 1 A) and its specific geographic location (e.g., Tanah Abang, Kota Jakarta Pusat).

    ![Inventory Name](/images/inventory-name.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Location Map">
    A mini-map showing the exact pinpoint location of the billboard. It often includes an icon representing the direction the screen is facing (see Direction Facing).

    ![Inventory Location](/images/inventory-location.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Price Estimation">
    Provides the estimated cost/rate card of a billboard for a specific duration (e.g., IDR 459.8M/mo).

    ![Inventory Price Estimation](/images/inventory-price-estimation.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="eCPM (Effective Cost Per Mille)">
    Displays the estimated cost to reach one thousand impressions. This is a crucial metric for evaluating the cost-efficiency of the inventory (e.g., IDR 801.8K).

    ![Inventory E CPM](/images/inventory-eCPM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Potential Impressions per Day">
    The estimated total number of times your advertisement could be seen by passing traffic within a single day.

    ![Inventory Potential Impressions](/images/inventory-potential-impressions.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Potential Reach per day">
    The estimated number of unique individuals who are likely to see your advertisement in a single day.

    ![Inventory Potential Reach](/images/inventory-potential-reach.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pricing">
    This section allows you to choose from available pricing options  (if applicable to the inventory).

    <Info>
      Each SKU has a different eCPM of the billboard.
    </Info>

    ![Inventory Select Pricing](/images/inventory-select-pricing.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inventory Specifications">
    ![Inventory Specification 1](/images/inventory-specification-1.png)

    A comprehensive breakdown of the billboard's physical and technical details. This includes:

    - **Type & Category:** Indicates the kind of asset (e.g., Digital Billboard) (see Specification, Type & Category).
    - **Orientation:** The layout format (e.g., Portrait) (see Orientation).
    - **Direction Facing:** The compass direction the screen points toward (e.g., West) (see Pointer & Direction Facing).
    - **Size:** The physical dimensions (e.g., 1m x 1.9m).
    - **Height from ground:** The vertical elevation of the screen (e.g., 5m).
    - **Total Unit:** The total number of screens available at this specific location (e.g., 20) (see Unit)
    - **Max Slot:** The total number of ad slots in the digital rotation (see Slot)
    - **Video Duration per spot:** The length of each ad play (e.g., 30s) (see Spot)
    - **Total Spot per slot:** The number of times your ad will play within a given period (e.g., 360).
    - **Operational Hours:** The daily schedule when the screen is active and displaying content (e.g., Monday - Sunday, 06:00:00 - 23:59:00 WIB).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Point of Interest (POI)">
    List significant nearby locations or contextual environment categories (like Commercial, Entertainment, or Transportation) that surround the billboard. This helps you understand the type of audience that frequents the area. 

    ![Inventory Poi](/images/inventory-poi.png)

    You can also expand the details of the POI Sub Category and name. 

    ![Detail Poi](/images/detail-poi.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to List / Shortlist ">
    The primary action button to save this specific inventory to your Shortlist for future campaign planning (see Add Inventory to Shortlist).

    ![Inventory Add To List Button](/images/inventory-add-to-list-button.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
