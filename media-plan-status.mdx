---
title: "Media Plan Status"
---

There are three statuses that a media plan can have throughout its lifecycle, including:

## Draft

This status indicates that the media plan is still in the preparation phase and has not yet been launched. During this stage, you are still building the plan, which means you can safely edit schedules, add inventories.

  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>

---

## Launched

This status indicates that the media plan has been officially approved and launched into the system. The media plan is now actively running or queued to run exactly according to your defined start and end dates.

<img
  src="/images/Media-Plan-Status---Launched.png"
  alt="Media Plan Status Launched"
  title="Media Plan Status Launched"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>

---

## Finished

This status indicates that the media plan has completed its operational lifecycle. A media plan reaches this status in **two ways:** **automatically when the established end date has passed**, or **manually if you choose to "force complete"** (see Force Complete Media Plan) **the media plan** to stop it before its scheduled end date.
