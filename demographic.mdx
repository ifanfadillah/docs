The Demographic section provides a comprehensive profile of the audience that typically passes by the selected inventory. This allows you to ensure the physical screen aligns perfectly with your target.

## Demographic Overview

![Demographic Overview](/images/demographic-overview.png)

Based on the **Demographic Overview** above, here is a detailed explanation of each section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Age Demographic">
    A bar chart breaking down the passing audience into specific age groups (e.g., 15-19, 20-24, 25-29).

    ![Age Demographic](/images/age-demographic.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Education Demographic:">
    A pie chart detailing the highest level of education of the audience (e.g., High School, Bachelor's Degree).

    ![Education Demographic](/images/education-demographic.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Occupation Demographic">
    A pie chart showing the primary professions of the people in that area (e.g., Students, Entrepreneurs, Civil Servants).

    ![Occupation Demographic](/images/occupation-demographic.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="SES (Socio-Economic Status)">
    A breakdown of the audience's economic class (e.g., Upper A, Middle B), helping you gauge purchasing power.

    ![Ses Segment](/images/ses-segment.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
