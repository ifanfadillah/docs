title: "Ads Verification"
---

To ensure absolute transparency and accountability in every campaign, Playlog provides a centralized ad verification system. This independent monitoring feature guarantees that advertisers get exactly what they paid for, combining real-world checks with digital precision directly within the Playlog dashboard.

Because Out-of-Home advertising utilizes both physical and digital assets, Playlog employs two distinct methodologies to verify that your ads are actively running:

![Ads Verification 1](/images/ads-verification-1.png)

## Static OOH Verification

Physical, printed billboards (Static OOH) cannot send digital signals back to a server to confirm they are still standing or in good condition.

- **The Method:** Playlog verifies static assets monthly through **3rd-party surveyors**.
- **How It Works:** Independent, on-the-ground surveyors are dispatched to the physical locations of the billboards. They take photographic evidence to confirm that the printed canvas is correctly installed, clearly visible, and undamaged by weather or wear. This proof of performance is then uploaded to the platform.

---

## Digital OOH (DOOH) Verification

Digital screens rotate multiple ads in a loop, making manual surveying inefficient for tracking every single play.

- **The Method:** Playlog verifies DOOH assets programmatically via an **HTML5 playback loop**.
- **How It Works:** Because the ad content is hosted directly on Playlog, the system can automatically track the HTML5 playback data. Every time the digital ad is successfully rendered and played on the publisher's physical screen, the system logs it. This provides highly accurate, real-time proof of play without relying solely on the publisher's own reporting.

**The Centralized Advantage** By integrating both physical survey data and digital playback logs into one unified interface, Playlog eliminates the need for advertisers to manually chase down publishers for "Proof of Play" reports. Everything is independently monitored and accessible from your centralized dashboard.
