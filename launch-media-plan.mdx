---
title: "Launch Media Plan"
---

After completing the media plan creation process—starting from scheduling, selecting inventory, adding creative content, and configuring reports—the next step is to launch the media plan.

Launching a Media Plan is the final execution step that indicates your campaign is ready to go live. Once this process is complete, the Campaign status will transition from **"Not Started"** to **"Running"**, and the Media Plan status will change from **"Draft"** to **"Launched"**. The system will automatically read the start time of all launched media plans in a campaign and begin recording performance metrics (Impressions, Reach, Frequency, etc.) when the flight date arrives.

<Info>
  **Important Note:** The launch process is carried out individually for each media plan. If you have three different media plans within a single campaign, you must carry out the launch process three separate times as each plan becomes ready for use.
</Info>

This page will help you launch a media plan for a campaign.

## Step: Click "Launch Media Plan" Button

Navigate to the specific media plan you want to execute and click the **Launch Media Plan** button located on the top right side of the media plan row.

![Launch Media Plan 1](/images/launch-media-plan-1.png)

After the media plan is successfully launched, its status badge will instantly change from **“Draft”** to **“Launched”**.

![Launch Media Plan 1 2](/images/launch-media-plan-1.2.png)

<Info>
  **Action Locked:** Please double-check your setup before launching. Once a Media Plan is officially launched, **you will no longer be able to change its schedule or modify/add the selected inventory**. All configurations are locked to ensure stable campaign delivery and reporting.
</Info>
