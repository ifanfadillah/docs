This page will help you with each section on the Detail Shortlist Page.

## Accessing Detail Shortlist

You can access the detailed shortlist by clicking the "**Shortlist Name**" Menu in the list.

![Accessing Detail Shortlist 1](/images/accessing-detail-shortlist-1.png)

Then it will display the **Detail Shortlist Page.**

![Accessing Detail Shortlist 1 2](/images/accessing-detail-shortlist-1.2.png)

## Detail Shortlist Overview

![Detail Shortlist Overview 2](/images/detail-shortlist-overview-2.png)

Based on the **Shortlist Detail Overview** above, here is a detailed explanation of each section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Shortlist Header & Information">
    This top section displays the designated title/name of the shortlist (e.g., Shortlist A). It features a visual tag indicating the current progress status, such as "In Progress," which means the shortlist is currently active and you are still in the planning phase (see Shortlist Status).

    It also provides the Date Created, Last Update timestamps, and the total number of individual physical screens saved within the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort By Dropdown">
    This is a dropdown menu that allows you to organize the displayed list of billboards. You can use this tool to quickly sort the inventory cards by specific criteria, making it easier to find screens that align with your goals.

    ![Detail Shortlist Short By](/images/detail-shortlist-short-by.png)

    By clicking the dropdown, you can arrange the inventory using the following specific filters:

    - **Highest / Lowest CPM:** Highlights the inventory based on Cost Per Mille, allowing you to sort by the most or least cost-efficient screens.
    - **Highest / Lowest Price:** Arranges the list starting with the most premium screens or the most budget-friendly screens based on their listed price.
    - **Highest / Lowest Impression:** Sorts the inventory to display billboards generating the highest or lowest total daily exposures.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grouping Dropdown (Inventory Type / City)">
    This is a filtering menu that allows you to organize and narrow down the displayed list of saved screens. By clicking this dropdown, you can choose how your curated inventory is categorized:

    <Accordion title="Inventory Type (Default)">
      Group your selected screens based on their specific asset type, such as separating digital displays (DOOH) from conventional screens (OOH).

      ![Detail Shortlist View Inventory Type](/images/detail-shortlist-view-inventory-type.png)
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="City">
      Group your saved screens geographically according to their specific city locations.

      <Steps>
        <Step title="Click Dropdown">
          ![Detail Shortlist View City 1](/images/detail-shortlist-view-city-1.png)
        </Step>
        <Step title='Select "City"'>
          ![Detail Shortlist View City 2](/images/detail-shortlist-view-city-2.png)

          Then it will group **inventory by City,** such as Kota Jakarta Pusat or Kota Jakarta Selatan.

          ![Detail Shortlist View City 2 2](/images/detail-shortlist-view-city-2.2.png)
        </Step>
        <Step title="Show Interactive Map">
          When you choose to group the list by "City", a "Map" toggle switch becomes available at the top right of the category header.

          ![Detail Shortlist View City 3](/images/detail-shortlist-view-city-3.png)

          Turning this toggle on activates an inline map that highlights the selected city's geographic boundaries and visually pinpoints the exact locations of your saved billboards within that area for spatial context.

          ![Detail Shortlist View City 3 2](/images/detail-shortlist-view-city-3.2.png)
        </Step>
      </Steps>
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Batch Selection Checkbox DOOH">
    This master checkbox allows you to select or deselect all individual inventory cards within that specific group at once. This is useful for performing batch actions, such as unselecting multiple inventories from your shortlist.

    ![Detail Shortlist Batch Selection Checkbox](/images/detail-shortlist-batch-selection-checkbox.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Batch Selection Checkbox OOH">
    This is a comprehensive card representing a single physical screen added to your shortlist. It provides a visual snapshot and key details of the asset, including an image, screen name, size, orientation, total available slots, pricing, and estimated performance metrics like impressions.

    ![Detail Shortlist Inventory Summary Card](/images/detail-shortlist-inventory-summary-card.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inventory Summary and Information">
    This is a comprehensive card representing a single physical screen added to your shortlist. It provides a visual snapshot and key details of the asset, including an image, screen name, size, orientation, total available slots, pricing, and estimated performance metrics like impressions.

    ![Detail Shortlist Inventory Summary Card 1](/images/detail-shortlist-inventory-summary-card-1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slot Availability Indicator & Selector">
    This section indicates how many standard DOOH Inventory slots are physically available within the rotation cycle of a digital screen (e.g., "8 Slot Available"). This helps you understand the screen's capacity for your ad campaign. Additionally, for DOOH screens, you can use the selector tool (\+ / -) in this section to add or adjust the number of slots you want to secure based on your specific campaign needs.

    ![Detail Shortlist Slot Availability](/images/detail-shortlist-slot-availability.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Rate Card">
    This action button (represented by a pencil icon) allows you to edit the rate card and modify the specific settings or booking details for that individual screen (see Edit Inventory Rate Card to Shortlist).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Inventory">
    This action button (represented by a trash bin icon) allows you to permanently remove the selected inventory card from your shortlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Individual Selection Checkbox">
    Located on each inventory summary card, this checkbox allows you to select that single specific billboard. The summary panel on the right only calculates totals for the individual items that you have checked.

    ![Detail Shortlist Individual Selection](/images/detail-shortlist-individual-selection.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Total Estimation Impression">
    It calculates key metrics like **Total Impression** (combined daily exposures).

    ![Detail Shortlist Est Impression](/images/detail-shortlist-est-impression.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Campaign Parameter Summary Panel">
    Located on the right side of the page, this panel displays a combined summary of the entire shortlist. It calculates key metrics like\*\* DOOH & OOH Price Estimation\*\* and the **Total Price Estimation** for everything currently selected in your curated list.

    ![Detail Shortlist Summary Panel](/images/detail-shortlist-summary-panel.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Proposal Button">
    Once you are satisfied with the curated inventory in your shortlist, clicking this button initiates the process of exporting the data into a media proposal document that can be shared with clients for review and final approval (see Export Proposal).

    ![Detail Shortlist Export Proposal](/images/detail-shortlist-export-proposal.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Status Button">
    Every status update (from _In Progress_, _Under Review_, to _Completed_) must be changed manually by the user according to the actual progress of your campaign planning.

    <Accordion title="How to Update Shortlist">
      Here is how you can update the status of your Shortlist:

      <Steps>
        <Step title='Click "Update Status" Dropdown'>
          On the Shortlist Detail page, locate the summary panel on the right side. Click the **Update Status** dropdown menu to reveal the available options.

          ![Detail Shortlist Update Status 1](/images/detail-shortlist-update-status-1.png)
        </Step>
        <Step title="Select Status">
          ![Detail Shortlist Update Status 2](/images/detail-shortlist-update-status-2.png)

          From the dropdown list, select the status that reflects your current workflow phase. You can choose between:

          - **In Progress**
          - **Under Review**
          - **Completed**
        </Step>
        <Step title="Confirm the Update">
          ![Detail Shortlist Update Status 3](/images/detail-shortlist-update-status-3.png)

          Once confirmed, the system will display a green success notification stating "Successfully updated shortlist status" at the top of your screen. Additionally, the status badge located next to your Shortlist Name (at the top left) will instantly update to reflect your newly selected status.
        </Step>
      </Steps>
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert Campaign Button">
    After receiving final client approval on the proposed shortlist, clicking this button triggers the workflow to transform your finalized media plan directly into an active campaign reporting.

    ![Detail Shortlist Convert To Campaign Button](/images/detail-shortlist-convert-to-campaign-button.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
