## Landscape

The advertising screen is wider than it is tall (a horizontal format). This is the most common orientation for traditional, large-format roadside billboards.

## Portrait

The advertising screen is taller than it is wide (a vertical format). This orientation is frequently used for pedestrian-level displays, such as standing digital panels and transit screens.

## Undefined

The physical orientation of this specific asset has not yet been categorized or input into the system's database. Selecting this might show screens with missing specification data.
