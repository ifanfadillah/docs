---
title: "Add Inventory to Shortlist"
---

Once you have successfully created a Shortlist, you can begin populating it with inventory that aligns with your media plan. This feature allows you to seamlessly save inventories directly from the Explore page into your dedicated planning workspace.

## Step 1: Go to Explore

![Add To Shortlist Explore](/images/add-to-shortlist-explore.png)

---

## Step 2: Search Inventory

While browsing the map, filtering options, or viewing the detailed inventory list in the **Explore** feature, find a screen that meets your campaign requirements.

![Add To Shortlist Select](/images/add-to-shortlist-select.png)

---

## Step 3: Click "Add to List"

This is the primary action button used to save a specific inventory to your Shortlist for future campaign planning.

![Add To Shortlist 3](/images/add-to-shortlist-3.png)

After clicking “**Add to List**”, some Inventory will show the dropdown of SKU and estimated Price.

![Add To Shortlist 3 2](/images/add-to-shortlist-3.2.png)

After selecting one of the SKU list, a modal window titled “Convert from Shortlist” will appear on your screen.

![Add To Shortlist 3 3](/images/add-to-shortlist-3.3.png)

---

## Step 4: Select Shortlist

<Steps>
  <Step title='Browse or search for your pre-planned Shortlist (e.g., "Shortlist A")'>
    ![Add To Shortlist 4 1](/images/add-to-shortlist-4.1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Checkbox Button Next to the Desired Shortlist">
    ![Add To Shortlist 4 2](/images/add-to-shortlist-4.2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click the "Save" Button'>
    ![Add To Shortlist 4 3](/images/add-to-shortlist-4.3.png)

    Inventory successfully added to your Shortlist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
