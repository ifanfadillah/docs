---
title: "Force Complete"
---

This is another action from the dropdown action button that can be taken to forcibly terminate a media plan (media plan already has **“Launched”** status) if there is a problem in the field related to one of the inventories or other reasons that require you to terminate the media plan.When the **“Force Complete”** action is taken, the media plan status will change from **“Launched”** to **“Finished”**.

<Info>
  If you want to continue with a media plan that has been forced to end, you cannot reuse that media plan. Instead, you must create a new media plan.
</Info>

## Step 1: Click "Force Complete" Button

![Force Complete Media Plan 1](/images/force-complete-media-plan-1.png)

---

## Step 2: Click "Yes, Force Complete"

![Force Complete Media Plan 2](/images/force-complete-media-plan-2.png)

After clicking the **“Yes, Force Complete”** button, the media plan is successfully terminated and its status changes from **“Launched”** to **“Finished”**, even though the campaign period or duration is still running.

![Force Complete Media Plan 2 2](/images/force-complete-media-plan-2.2.png)
