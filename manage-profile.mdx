## How to Edit Profile

If there are changes to your account profile, you can change or edit it by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Update Your Information">
            On the Profile page, you can modify the available fields under the **Change Information** section. 
            
            Currently, you can only update the following **Profile Photo** & **Username**
            
            ![Manage Profile](/images/documentation/settings/manage-profile/edit-profile-1.png)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Save Changes">
            Once you have updated your Username or Photo, click the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the page to apply your new data.
            
            ![Manage Profile](/images/documentation/settings/manage-profile/edit-profile-2.png)
      </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
      Fields with a gray background such as **Email Address**, **Position**, and **User Role** are locked and cannot be edited manually. If you need to update these specific details, please contact the Playlog Support Team at **info@playlog.io**.
</Note>
