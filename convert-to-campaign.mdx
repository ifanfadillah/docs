---
title: "Convert to Campaign"
---

After your exported proposal has received final client approval, you can seamlessly convert your finalized Shortlist into an active, live media plan for your new campaign.

## Step 1: Click "Convert to Campaign"

When your curated list of inventories is finalized, click the **Convert to Campaign** button.

![Convert To Campaign 1](/images/convert-to-campaign-1.png)

This will initiate the process of turning your Shortlist into a campaign media buy.

![Convert To Campaign 1 2](/images/convert-to-campaign-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Configure Campaign Information

This section establishes the foundational identity and flight dates of your campaign media buy.

![Convert To Campaign 2](/images/convert-to-campaign-2.png)

Based on the form above, the information or data required to create a new campaign is as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Campaign Name (Required)">
    You can enter the name of the campaign you created or manage.

    <Info>
      The campaign name you use cannot be duplicated. When you enter a campaign name that has been used previously and still exists in the system, the system will issue a warning that the campaign name cannot be used.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duration (Required)">
    Click the calendar icon to define your **Start Date** and **End Date**. The system will automatically calculate the **Total Days** of your campaign flight period.

    <Info>
      The selected campaign duration will be used when you filter the Campaign Report page. The date range that will be active as a filter on the campaign report page is the range of the campaign duration.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Name (Optional)">
    You can fill in this field with the brand name associated with the campaign you are creating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Image (Optional)">
    You can upload photos from brands related to the campaign you are going to create.

    <Info>
      Image formats are: .JPG, .JPEG, and .PNG with a minimum size of 300 x 300 px (optimal size is 700 x 700 px).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 3: Click "Save & Continue"

Once you have filled out all the necessary information, click the **Save & Continue** button to finalize the conversion process and move to the next campaign setup stage.

![Convert To Campaign 3](/images/convert-to-campaign-3.png)

After completing the campaign creation process, you can continue to set up your media plan (see Create Media Plan).
