## How to Change Password if Forgot
If you forget your password, you can reset it by following these steps:
<Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the Login Page">
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the 'Forgot your Password' button">
            ![Forgot Password 1](/images/documentation/getting-started/access/forgot-password/Forgot%20Password%20-%201.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your Email Address to request a password reset">
            ![Forgot Password 2](/images/documentation/getting-started/access/forgot-password/Forgot%20Password%20-%202.png)           
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the 'Send Password Reset Link' button">
            ![Forgot Password 3](/images/documentation/getting-started/access/forgot-password/Forgot%20Password%20-%203.png)        

            You will receive a password reset link via email. Check your Inbox or Spam/Junk folder to reset your password.

            ![Forgot Password 4](/images/documentation/getting-started/access/forgot-password/Forgot%20Password%20-%204.png)
      </Step> 
</Steps>

## Login Failed
If you fail to log in due to an error—either system-related or external—you can try resetting your password by clicking the **Forgot your Password** button. If the issue persists, please contact the Lynx SSP internal team for further assistance.
