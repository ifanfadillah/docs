If you are accessing the Lynx SSP Platform for the first time, please contact our internal team via agelius.garetta@lynxssp.com by providing your email address for access. The Lynx SSP team will then create an account for you and your associated Organization. You will be contacted again with login credentials **(Email and Password)** that you can use to access the Lynx SSP Platform.
