Welcome to **Lynx SSP!**

Lynx SSP is designed as a solution for Media Owners to manage campaigns and inventory more easily, efficiently, and in an organized way—whether for OOH (Out-of-Home) or DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home). 

With Lynx SSP, you as a Media Owner can systematically record inventory data, create, manage, and monitor advertising campaigns that are running or upcoming. Equipped with efficient campaign, inventory, and reporting management features, Lynx SSP helps you achieve greater effectiveness, reach your goals, and enhance efficiency in executing your campaign strategies.

---

## What Next?

As the OOH (Out-of-Home) and DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) advertising markets continue to grow, Lynx SSP keeps innovating. With the rapid expansion of digital DOOH inventory and the rise of programmatic campaign transactions, Lynx SSP is continuously enhancing its Campaign Management System (CMS) to support both regular and programmatic campaigns, complete with asset inventory management and reporting features. These innovations are designed to improve user experience and position Lynx SSP as a unified Campaign Management System (CMS) and Supply-Side Platform (SSP) that is more efficient and effective in helping Media Owners achieve their goals and maximize asset revenue.
