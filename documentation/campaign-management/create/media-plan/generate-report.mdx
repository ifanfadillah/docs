---
title: "Generate Report"
---

You can activate the **"Generate Report"** button if the media plan and campaign you are managing require reporting ([See Campaign Report ↗]()).

![Chrome Capture 2025 10 15 Gi](/images/chrome-capture-2025-10-15.gif)

<Note>
  There are **limitations or restrictions** for you as the media owner regarding report generation. The available slots are subject to the **subscription agreement** with the Lynx SSP team.
</Note>

You can disable report generation or keep it enabled and proceed to launch your media plan ([See Launch Media Plan ↗](/documentation/campaign-management/create/media-plan/launch)).
