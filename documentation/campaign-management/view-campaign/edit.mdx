---
title: "Edit a Campaign"
---

If there are changes to the information in your campaign, you can change or edit it by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the 'Action' Button & Click 'Edit'">
            This will display the campaign information details page that will be changed or edited.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Save & Continue” Button ">
            Once you have finished making changes, click the “Save & Continue” button to save the changed data.

            You will then receive a notification that the campaign data has been successfully edited.
      </Step>
</Steps>
