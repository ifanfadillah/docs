This page helps you manage members.

## Members Management Overview

![Members Management Overview](/images/documentation/settings/members-management/members-management-overview.png)

Based on the **Members Management Overview** above, here is an explanation of each section:

### Member Search
You can search for member data by name.

---

### Filter by Position
You can filter or sort the list of members based on their position in your organization.

---

### List Member
In this section, you can see a list of all members who belong to the same organization as you. The properties that can be viewed in this table are as follows:

- Member name
- Phone number
- Position
- Role
- Last login

![List Members](/images/documentation/settings/members-management/members-management-list.png)

---

### Delete Member Button
You can remove members from the list by pressing the **"Delete"** button.

![Delete Member Button](/images/documentation/settings/members-management/members-management-delete-button.png)

---

### Pagination
The sequential numbers in the image below are navigation links you can use to go to the next member list page.

![Pagination](/images/documentation/settings/members-management/members-management-pagination.png)

---

### Page List
You can change the list of members displayed. By default, it is set to 10 items, but you can change it to 50 or 100. 

![Page List](/images/documentation/settings/members-management/members-management-page-list.png)
