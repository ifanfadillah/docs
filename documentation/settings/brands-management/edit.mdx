---
title: "Edit Brand"
---

If there are changes to your brand, you can change or edit it by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Edit” Button on the Selected Brand">

            ![Edit Brand 1](/images/documentation/settings/brands-management/edit/edit-brand-1.png)

            Then it will display the details of the selected brand.
      
            ![Edit Brand 1.2](/images/documentation/settings/brands-management/edit/edit-brand-1.2.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Save” Button">
            Once you have finished making changes, press the **“Save”** button to save the changed data.

            ![Edit Brand 2](/images/documentation/settings/brands-management/edit/edit-brand-2.png)

            You will then receive a notification that the brand has been successfully changed or edited.
      </Step>
</Steps>
