---
title: "Edit Filler Content "
---

If there are changes to the specifications of your filler content, you can change or edit it by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the 'Action' Button & Click 'Edit'">
      
      ![Edit Filler Content 1](/images/documentation/creative-management/filler-content/edit/edit-filler-content-1.png)

      This will display the filler content detail page to be edited.

      ![Edit Filler Content 1](/images/documentation/creative-management/filler-content/edit/edit-filler-content-1.2.png)
            
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the 'Save' Button">
      Once you have finished making changes, press the **Save** button to save the changed data.

      ![Edit Filler Content 2](/images/documentation/creative-management/filler-content/edit/edit-filler-content-2.png)

      You will then receive a notification that the filler content data has been successfully edited.

      ![Edit Filler Content 2.2]
      </Step>
</Steps>
