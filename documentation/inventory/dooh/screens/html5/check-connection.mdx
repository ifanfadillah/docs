This page helps you check the running filer in your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory after connect using HTML5.

## How to Check Connection

---

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Action” Button & Click “Edit”">
            ![How to Check Connection 1](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/html5/check-connection/check-connection-1.png)
            
            This will display the **Inventory Details** Page.

            ![How to Check Connection 1.2](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/html5/check-connection/check-connection-1.2.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Screen” Tab">
            ![How to Check Connection 2](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/html5/check-connection/check-connection-2.png)
         
            Then it will display the Screen Page.

            ![How to Check Connection 2.2](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/html5/check-connection/check-connection-2.2.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click “Copy URL” ">
            ![How to Check Connection 3](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/html5/check-connection/check-connection-3.png)


      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the URL in a browser tab ">
            Then it will display the added filler.
            
      </Step>
</Steps>

You can monitor the list of creatives and fillers that have been played on your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory through ([See Playlist & Playlog ↗](/documentation/inventory/dooh/playlist-playlog/overview)).
