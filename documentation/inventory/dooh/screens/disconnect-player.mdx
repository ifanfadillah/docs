You can also disconnect DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory from the screen by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Disconnect Player” Button">
            
            ![Disconnect Player 1](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/disconnect-player/disconnect-player-1.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Yes, Confirm” Button">
            
            ![Disconnect Player 2](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/disconnect-player/disconnect-player-2.png)

            This successfully disconnects the player from the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory to the screen.
      </Step>
</Steps>
