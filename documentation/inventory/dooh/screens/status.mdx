---
title: "Screen Status"
---

By default, the screen status of DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory after being successfully added is **“Not Connected”**. However, after being successfully configured or connected, there are several screen statuses according to the type of connection used. The following are the types of screen statuses, including: 

## Screen App
If you use the Screen App, the following are the types of Screen Status:

### Not Connected
Not Connected indicates that the Screen App has not been connected.

<img
  src="/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/status/status-screen-app-not-connected.png"
  alt="status-screen-app-not-connected"
  title="status-screen-app-not-connected"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"20%" }}
/>

### Offline
Offline indicates that Screen App has been successfully connected, but the device is turned off.

<img
  src="/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/status/status-screen-app-offline.png"
  alt="status-screen-app-offline"
  title="status-screen-app-offline"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"15%" }}
/>


### Online
Online indicates that the Screen App has been successfully connected, and the device is turned on and ready to use.

<img
  src="/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/screens/status/status-screen-app-online.png"
