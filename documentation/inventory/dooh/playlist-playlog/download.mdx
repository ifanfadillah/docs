title: "Download Playlog"
---

You can download playlog data to provide log data from creatives that are displayed on your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory.

This page helps you with the steps to download playlog data, including:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Download” Button">

      ![ Download Playlog 1 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/playlist-playlog/download/download-playlog-1.png)
           
            Then a pop-up will appear.

      ![ Download Playlog 1.2 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/playlist-playlog/download/download-playlog-1.2.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Log Period (Required)">
            
      ![ Download Playlog 2 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/playlist-playlog/download/download-playlog-2.png)
            
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Member Email Addresses in Your Organization (Required)">

      ![ Download Playlog 3 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/playlist-playlog/download/download-playlog-3.png)
            
            The email address you selected will receive log data in the form of a .csv file later.
      
      </Step>
      <Step title=" Click the “Submit” Button">
            
      ![ Download Playlog 4 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/dooh/playlist-playlog/download/download-playlog-4.png)
            
      </Step>
</Steps>
