---
title: "Occupancy Rate"
---

Occupancy Rate is a display of the occupancy of your media plan and total media plan currently running on your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory. Occupancy Rate helps you understand how much space or opportunity is left for additional use in your current media plan.

This page helps you understand the Occupancy Rate display for DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory on the Lynx SSP Platform. 

## Accessing Occupancy Rate
<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Action” Button & Click “Edit”">
            
            This will display the **Inventory Details Page.**

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click “Occupancy Rate” Tab">
            
            This will display the Occupancy Rate Page.

      </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Occupancy Rate Overview

Based on the **Occupancy Rate Overview** above, here is an explanation of each section:

### Search Campaign or Media Plan
You can search by typing keywords based on the name of the campaign or media plan that fills your inventory.

---

### Filter by Campaign Type 
You can filter the list of campaigns or media plans that fill your inventory based on campaign type, namely **Regular** or **Exclusive** ([See Campaign Type ↗](/documentation/campaign-management/campaign/type)).

---

### Filter by Campaign Status
You can filter to sort the list of campaigns or media plans that fill your inventory based on their campaign status ([See Campaign Status ↗](/documentation/campaign-management/campaign/status)).

---

### Filter by Campaign Period
You can filter the list of campaigns or media plans that fill your inventory based on the period. By default, the period displays campaign data for the last 30 days.

---

### List Campaign
In this section, you can see all the campaign and media plan lists that fill your inventory. The playlist data is displayed based on filtering by type, status, and period (Points 2, 3, and 4). The properties that can be seen in this table are as follows:

- Campaign & Media Plan Name
- Period
- Status
- Campaign Type

---

### Campaign Count Information
Displays information on the number of campaigns in your inventory. The playlist data is displayed based on filtering by type, status, and period (Points 2, 3, and 4).

---

### Pagination
The sequential numbers in the image below are navigation points you can use to go to the next campaign list page.

---

### Page List
You can change the list of campaigns displayed. By default, there are 10 items, and you can change this to 50 or 100.
