title: "Schedule"
---

Schedule is a page that displays a list of campaign durations for each inventory you own. Through the schedule, you can see the availability of your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) and OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory.

This page helps you understand the Schedule menu in the Inventory feature on the Lynx SSP Platform.

## Schedule Overview

![ Schedule Overview ](/images/documentation/inventory/schedule/schedule-overview.png)

Based on the **Schedule Overview** above, here is an explanation of each section:

### Filter by Date
You can filter the campaign schedule data displayed on this page based on the date you want in the format dd/MM/yyyy.

---

### Filter by Inventory
You can filter the campaign schedule data displayed on this page based on the inventory you select.

---

### Filter by Media Plan Status
You can filter the campaign schedule data displayed on this page based on the media plan status you select. 

---

### Search Inventory
You can also search by inventory name or registered SKU.

---

### Calendar
Date order for displaying inventory availability list.

![ Calendar ](/images/documentation/inventory/schedule/schedule-calendar.png)

---

### List Inventory
Displaying the Inventory List and each slot owned.

![ List Inventory ](/images/documentation/inventory/schedule/schedule-list-inventory.png)

---

### List Availability Slot
Display a list of available slots for each inventory item based on date.

**Example:** If the slot list displays **“5/9”**, it means that the inventory has **9 slots**, **5 slots** are already filled, and **4 slots** are still available.

![ List Availability Slot ](/images/documentation/inventory/schedule/schedule-list-availability-slot.png)

---

### List Campaign & Media Plan Occupancy
Display a list of campaigns and media plans that fill each slot of the available inventory.

![ List Campaign & Media Plan Occupancy ](/images/documentation/inventory/schedule/schedule-list-campaign-and-media-plan-occupancy.png)
