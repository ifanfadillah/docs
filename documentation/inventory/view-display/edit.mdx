---
title: "Edit a Display"
---

If there are changes to the specifications of your inventory, you can change or edit them by following these steps:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click the “Action” Button & Click “Edit”">

            ![ Edit a Display 1 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/view-display/edit/edit-inventory-display-1.png)

            This will display the Inventory details page that will be changed or edited.
      
            ![ Edit a Display 1.2 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/view-display/edit/edit-inventory-display-1.2.png)

      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Save” Button">
            Once you have finished making changes, press the **“Save”** button to save the changed data.

            ![ Edit a Display 2 ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/view-display/edit/edit-inventory-display-2.png)

            You will then receive a notification that the inventory data has been successfully edited.
      </Step>
</Steps>
