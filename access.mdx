Brand Management is a feature that allows you to manage brand data for your organization.

This page helps you manage brand data.

## Brand Management Overview

![Brands Management Overview](/images/brands-management-overview.png)

Based on the **Brand Management Overview** above, here is an explanation of each section:

### Search Brand

You can search for brand data by name.

![Brands Management Search](/images/brands-management-search.png)

---

### Filter by Industry

You can filter the brand data that appears in the list based on its industry.

![Brands Management Filter By Industry](/images/brands-management-filter-by-industry.png)

---

### Add New Brand Button

You can create a new brand by pressing the **“Add New”** button.

![Brands Management Add New Button](/images/brands-management-add-new-button.png)

---

### List Brand

In this section, you can see a list of all brands within the same organization as you. The properties that can be viewed in this table are as follows:

- Brand Name
- Description
- Member

![Brands Management List](/images/brands-management-list.png)

---

### Action Menu "Edit" & "Invite Members"

You can change brand data through the **“Edit”** menu and add or invite members using the **“Invite Member”** menu.

![Brands Management Action Menu](/images/brands-management-action-menu.png)
