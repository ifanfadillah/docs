## What is OOH Media Measurement

Out-of-Home (OOH) media measurement is the industry standard for quantifying the audience exposed to physical advertising assets, such as billboards, transit screens, and retail displays. Unlike digital advertising where a click or view is tracked on a personal device, OOH measurement relies on understanding real-world human movement and traffic patterns to estimate how many people actually see an advertisement in the physical world.

## Playlog Measurement

The foundation of OOH measurement globally relies on three primary metrics. These metrics interact with each other to determine the total campaign delivery, which is calculated simply as: **Reach x Frequency = Impression**.

### Reach

The number of unique individuals that will likely see your ads regardless of frequency.

### Frequency

Is the average number of times the ads are seen by the unique population reached.

### Impression

The total number of exposures to your advertisement.
