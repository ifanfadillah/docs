Welcome to Playlog! Currently, new account creation is managed by our Support Team based on Your Billing to ensure your profile is securely and correctly linked to your Organization's workspace.

If you are ii.Playlog for the first time, please follow the steps below to get started.

## How to Request Access

To request a new account, you need to reach out to our team directly.

1. **Send an Email:** Contact the Playlog Support Team at [**info@playlog.io**](mailto:info@playlog.io)**[↗](https://playlog-44bde5a1.mintlify.app/login)** .
2. **Provide Your Information:** Please include the following details in your request to help us set up your workspace:
   - Your Full Name
   - Your Work Email Address
   - Your Organization or Company Name
   - Your Position

<Tip>
  We highly recommend using your official company email address rather than a personal one (like Gmail) to speed up the verification and approval process.
</Tip>

---

## What Happens Next?

Once our team receives and verifies your request, we will provision an account for you and associate it with your Organization.

**Welcome Email:** You will receive an automated email from Playlog containing your login credentials, including a **User ID / Email** & **Temporary Password**.
