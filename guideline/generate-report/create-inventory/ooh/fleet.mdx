This page helps you add new OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory with Fleet Type on the Lynx SSP Platform.

## How to Add Your Fleet OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory

When adding OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory to the Lynx SSP Platform, you will need to provide some information or data that you can prepare and enter in the following form:

 ![ Fleet ](/images/documentation/inventory/display/ooh/add/fleet/ooh-add-fleeet.png)

Based on the form above, the information or data required to add new OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory is as follows:

<Steps>
      <Step title="Display Name (Required)">
            You can enter the Display Name or unique name of your OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory.
      </Step>
      <Step title="SKU (Required)">
            There are no set rules regarding SKU numbering. It all depends on your policy for determining the format and numbering of SKUs. SKUs can be used as unique parameters for performing specific searches on inventory.

            <Info>
                  The **Display Name** & **SKU**  ou use cannot be duplicated. When you enter a Display Name & SKU that has been used previously and still exists in the system, the system will issue a warning that cannot be used.
            </Info>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Specification (Required)">
            You can choose the specifications according to the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory specifications that you have, **Fleet** ([See Specification, Type & Category ↗](/documentation/inventory/specification-type-and-category)).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type (Required)">
            The type you selected is **“OOH”** because it adds OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory.      
      </Step>
      <Step title="Category (Required)">
            You must select the category according to the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory that you will add.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Lighting (Required)">
            When you select Mobile Showcase (OOH) as the billboard type and category, you must provide information about the type of lighting used on the billboard. The available options include:
      
            <Accordion
                  title="Frontline"
            >
                   The lighting position is in front and directed at the billboard media.
            </Accordion>
            <Accordion
                  title="Backlight"
            >
                  The lighting position is behind and directed at the billboard media.
            </Accordion>
            <Accordion
                  title="No Light"
            >
                  Billboards do not use lighting at all.
            </Accordion>
            <Accordion
                  title="Undefined"
            >
                  Undefined
            </Accordion>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Height (Required)">
            You must enter the height of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory that you are adding. The unit of measurement is meters.

            **Example:**  a Mobile Showcase with specification **5 m x 5 m**. So entered **5 m as the height**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Width (Required)">
            You must enter the width of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory that you are adding. The unit of measurement is meters. 
           
            **Example:** a Mobile Showcase with specification **5 m x 5 m**. So entered **5 m as the width**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Schedules (Operational Days and Hours) (Required)">
            You must select specific operating days & hours from the Fleet OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard that you add to Lynx SSP            
      
            **Example:**  a Mobile Showcase with **All Operational Days** and operating hours only from **06.00 - 23.59**. 
      </Step>
      <Step title="Area (Required)">
            Defines the operational coverage area where the fleet display primarily operates or is most frequently visible.

            **Example:** Jakarta, Surabaya, Bali, etc.         
      </Step>
      <Step title="Address Notes (Required)">
            You can fill in this note with the complete address of your inventory location.           
      </Step>
      <Step title="Price (Required)">
            You must enter the monthly price or rate card of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard that you add to Lynx SSP.     
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload Display Image (Optional)">
            You can upload photos of OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboards that you have added to Lynx SSP.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Allow Regular Campaign (Optional)">
            You can specify or activate that your Fleet OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory is available for Regular campaign types ([See Select Inventory in the Media Plan ↗](/guideline/generate-report/create-campaign/create/media-plan/inventories/select)).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Potential Impressions per day (Optional)">
            If you have data on how many potential impressions per day are generated from the inventory, you can fill it in this column. But if not, you can leave it blank.
     
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the “Save” Button">
            After filling in all the information and adding OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory, click the **“Save”** button.
      </Step>
</Steps>
