## How to Approve Creative

You can approve creatives from Advertisers to run programmatic campaigns on your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory through the following steps: 

<Steps>
      <Step title="Click 'View Detail' Button">
      ![Approve Creative](/images/programmatic/creative-approval/Approve%20Creative%20-%201.png)
            
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review Detail Creative">
      ![Approve Creative](/images/programmatic/creative-approval/Approve%20Creative%20-%202.png)

      <Info>
            Make sure you have reviewed all the information from the creative before making a decision to Approve or Reject, because once approved, you cannot change the status back!
      </Info>
            
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the 'Approve' button">
      ![Approve Creative](/images/programmatic/creative-approval/Approve%20Creative%20-%203.png)
            
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the 'Approve' button">
      ![Approve Creative](/images/programmatic/creative-approval/Approve%20Creative%20-%204.png)
            
      </Step>
</Steps>

Then Creative was successfully approved and its status became **Approved**
