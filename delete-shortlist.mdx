---
title: "Delete Shortlist"
---

You can delete a shortlist by following these steps:

## Step 1: Click the "Action" Menu & Click "Delete"

![Delete Shortlist 1](/images/delete-shortlist-1.png)

Located at the far right of each shortlist entry, click the action menu (three dots) and select **Delete**.

---

## Step 2: Click the "Yes, Remove" Button

![Delete Shortlist 2](/images/delete-shortlist-2.png)

A confirmation modal will appear. Click the **"Yes, Remove"** button to confirm the removal.

You will then receive a notification that the shortlist has been successfully deleted.
