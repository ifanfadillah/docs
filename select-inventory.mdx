title: "Select Inventory"
---

This page will help you understand the **Inventories Tab** and how to add or select the inventory that a campaign’s media plan will use to run the creative advertisements.

Follow these steps to select and assign your target inventory.

## Step 1: Click "Add New" Inventory Button

Make sure you are viewing the **Inventories** tab under your newly scheduled media plan. Click the purple **"Add New"** button located on the right side of the screen.

![Select Inventory 1](/images/select-inventory-1.png)

Then the system will display a modal where you can search and select the inventory you want to use in your media plan.

![Select Inventory 1 2](/images/select-inventory-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Inventory Source

This modal gives you two distinct methods (Categories) for finding and selecting screens, depending on how you prepared your media plan.

<Accordion title="Option A: List by Shortlist">
  If you or your team have already explored inventory locations and saved them to a shortlist, this is the fastest way to add them.

  <Steps>
    <Step title='Click "Convert from Shortlist" '>
      ![Convert From Shortlist 1](/images/convert-from-shortlist-1.png)

      A modal window titled "**Convert from Shortlist**" will appear on your screen

      ![Convert From Shortlist 1 2](/images/convert-from-shortlist-1.2.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title='Browse or search for your pre-planned Shortlist (e.g., "Shortlist A")'>
      ![Convert From Shortlist 2](/images/convert-from-shortlist-2.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select the Radio Button Next to the Desired Shortlist">
      ![Convert From Shortlist 3](/images/convert-from-shortlist-3.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title='Click "Convert" Button'>
      ![Convert From Shortlist 4](/images/convert-from-shortlist-4.png)

      This will display back the **Inventory** Tab.

      ![Convert From Shortlist 4 2](/images/convert-from-shortlist-4.2.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title='Click "Add New" Inventory Button'>
      ![Convert From Shortlist 5](/images/convert-from-shortlist-5.png)

      This section will show list of inventory has been you added on Shortlist

      ![Convert From Shortlist 5 2](/images/convert-from-shortlist-5.2.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title='Click "Add" Inventory to List'>
      ![Convert From Shortlist 6](/images/convert-from-shortlist-6.png)

      This section contains a list of billboards that you have selected and can choose to use in your media plan.

      ![Convert From Shortlist 6 2](/images/convert-from-shortlist-6.2.png)

      <Info>
        An inventory card in the Selected Inventory section indicates that you are taking 1 slot from an inventory. When there are 2 cards with the same inventory name, it indicates that you are taking 2 slots from that inventory.
      </Info>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

## Step 3: Review and Click "Save"

Before finalizing, review the **Selected Inventories** column to ensure you have targeted the correct screens. You can also check the total count of screens at the bottom left of the modal (e.g., _Selected: 3 Inventories_).

![Select Inventory 3](/images/select-inventory-3.png)

Then the system will display a list of inventory that has been added.

![Select Inventory 3 2](/images/select-inventory-3.2.png)

You also can expand the display category to show Inventory item.

![Select Inventory 3 3](/images/select-inventory-3.3.png)
