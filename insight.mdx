Located at the bottom of the Insight tab, the Performance section visualizes the actual exposure data of the billboard over time.

## Insight Overview

![Insight Overview](/images/insight-overview.png)

Based on the **Insight Overview** above, here is a detailed explanation of each section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Time Filters">
    You can adjust the graph to show data by **Month, Week, Day, or Hour** to identify the absolute best times your ad will be seen.

    ![Insight Time Filter](/images/insight-time-filter.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Total Impressions">
    The cumulative number of times the billboard was exposed to the audience within your selected time period.

    ![Insight Impression Total](/images/insight-impression-total.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Peak Impression">
    The highest traffic point or maximum number of exposures achieved, helping you identify the absolute busiest times for this location.

    ![Insight Peak Impression](/images/insight-peak-impression.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Average Impression per day">
    The standard baseline metric shows the average daily audience exposures the screen typically generates.

    ![Insight Avg Impression](/images/insight-avg-impression.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trend Chart">
    A line graph visualizes the daily or hourly traffic trends.

    ![Insight Impression Performance](/images/insight-impression-performance.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
