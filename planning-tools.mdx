In the Playlog ecosystem, the **Planning Tools** module serves as the strategic foundation for media buyers, agencies, and direct advertisers. Because Playlog operates as a comprehensive demand planning and execution platform, this module is designed to provide data-driven precision and absolute transparency as you discover inventory and build your Out-of-Home (OOH) campaigns before they go live in the real world.
