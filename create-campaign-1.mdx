title: "Create Campaign"
---

Creating a campaign is the core action where your media planning becomes a reality. As an Agency or Media Buyer, this is where you will define the parameters of your ad buy, allocate your budget, select your target inventory, and upload your creative assets to be displayed on physical screens. Before you begin, ensure you have your media plan, approved budgets, flight dates, and final creative assets ready.

There are several steps involved in creating a campaign on the Playlog:

## Step 1: Click "Add New" Campaign

![Create Campaign 1](/images/create-campaign-1.png)

After click "Add New" Campaign Button, system will show the dropdown of choose your creation method **"Create Campaign"** or **"Convert from Shortlist".**

![Create Campaign 1 2](/images/create-campaign-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Creation Method

<Accordion title="Option 1: Create Campaign">

  </Steps>

## Step 3: Fill in Basic Information

Whether you chose to start from scratch or convert a shortlist, you will be directed to the **Informations \> Basic Information** page. This section establishes the foundational identity and flight dates of your media buy.

![Create Campaign 3](/images/create-campaign-3.png)

Based on the form above, the information or data required to create a new campaign is as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Campaign Name (Required)">
    You can enter the name of the campaign you created or manage.

    <Accordion title="Note: Convert from Shortlist">
      If you used the "Convert from Shortlist" method, this field will be pre-filled with the name of your shortlist (e.g., "Shortlist A From Shortlist"), but you can edit it as needed.

      ![Create Campaign 3 2 1](/images/create-campaign-3.2-1.png)
    </Accordion>

    <Info>
      The campaign name you use cannot be duplicated. When you enter a campaign name that has been used previously and still exists in the system, the system will issue a warning that the campaign name cannot be used.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duration (Required)">
    Click the calendar icon to define your **Start Date** and **End Date**. The system will automatically calculate the **Total Days** of your campaign flight period.

    <Info>
      The selected campaign duration will be used when you filter the Campaign Report page. The date range that will be active as a filter on the campaign report page is the range of the campaign duration.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Name (Optional)">
    You can fill in this field with the brand name associated with the campaign you are creating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand Image (Optional)">
    You can upload photos from brands related to the campaign you are going to create.

    <Info>
      Image formats are: .JPG, .JPEG, and .PNG with a minimum size of 300 x 300 px (optimal size is 700 x 700 px).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 4: Save & Continue

Once you have verified that the Basic Information aligns with your media plan, click the **Save & Continue** button at the bottom of the page.

![Create Campaign 4](/images/create-campaign-4.png)

This will save your progress as a Draft (**Status: "Not Started")** and move you to the next phase of the campaign setup or Media Plan (see Create Media Plan).

![Create Campaign 4 1](/images/create-campaign-4.1.png)
