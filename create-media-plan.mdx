---
title: "Add Media Plan"
---

Once your campaign's basic information is configured, the next crucial step is to create a **Media Plan**. In the media buying ecosystem, the media plan acts as the structural group for your buy allowing you to mapping out specific flight schedules and group the physical inventories that will deliver your ad content.

Follow these steps to generate a new media plan within your campaign framework:

## Step 1: Click the "Add Media Plan"

Navigate to the Media Plan tab within your campaign setup and click the **Add Media Plan** button.

![Add Media Plan 1](/images/add-media-plan-1.png)

This action will open an input modal where you can define the name of your new media plan.

![Add New Media Plan 1 2](/images/add-new-media-plan-1.2.png)

---

## Step 2: Fill the Media Plan Name

Input a clear and descriptive name for your media plan in the required text field.

![Add New Media Plan 2](/images/add-new-media-plan-2.png)

<Info>
  **Best Practice:** Use a structured and neat naming convention (e.g., _Phase 1 - Jakarta Static_ or _Weekend DOOH Takeover_) to keep your campaign architecture easy to navigate later on.
</Info>

---

## Step 3: Save Your Media Plan Set up

Once the name is entered, click the **Save Changes** button on the right side of the row.

![Add New Media Plan 3](/images/add-new-media-plan-3.png)

The system will instantly generate the media plan and add it to your campaign dashboard.

![Add New Media Plan 3 2](/images/add-new-media-plan-3.2.png)

---

## Step 4: Select Schedule

After successfully creating a media plan, its default status is **“Draft”** (see Media Plan Status). You can continue configuring the media plan by select the schedule or period of Your Media Plan.

![Add New Media Plan 4](/images/add-new-media-plan-4.png)

Once selected, the dates will be locked in and displayed as text next to a small edit (pencil) icon. You can click this pencil icon later if you need to adjust the flight dates.

![Add New Media Plan 4 2](/images/add-new-media-plan-4.2.png)

## Next Steps

Setting the schedule is the key that unlocks the rest of your media plan. Once your flight dates are successfully inputted, the **\+ Add New** button in the Inventories section will become fully active. 

With the timeline secured, you are now ready to move on to the core of media buying: selecting the inventory that will display your campaign during this scheduled timeframe (See Select Inventory).
